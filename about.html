About Us

 

The Uganda Squash Rackets Association (USRA) is dedicated to the growth and development of squash across Uganda. We support athletes, host tournaments, and offer youth engagement programs that spread the love of the sport nationwide.

 

Our Mission

 

To inspire participation in the sport, enhance player performance, and cultivate a lifelong passion for the sport, contributing to the health and wellbeing of society.

 

Our Vision

 

To be a leading force in making Squash a widely recognized and accessible sport throughout the Republic of Uganda. We aim to create a dynamic and inclusive environment wherein the sport thrives at both grassroots and professional levels.

 

Our Slogan

 

OWN THE COURT   |   MASTER THE GAME

 

Our Programs

 
       
  • Youth Development: Training and mentoring young athletes to excel in squash.
    •    
  • Competitive Tournaments: Hosting national and regional championships.
    •    
  • Community Outreach: Promoting squash in schools and local communities.
    •    
  • Coaching Clinics: Providing professional coaching and workshops.
